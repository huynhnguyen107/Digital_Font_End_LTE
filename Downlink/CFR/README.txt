The design performs 4× interpolation from 30.72 Msps to 122.88 Msps, followed by magnitude-based CFR. The CFR block uses CORDIC, Divider Generator, and Multiplier Generator IPs to compute the complex envelope, derive a Q1.15 gain, and scale the I/Q samples. The AXI DMA-based DUC+CFR chain was tested on a full LTE 20 MHz slot with 15360 input IQ samples, and the KR260 output matched the MATLAB fixed-point golden reference bit-exactly.
